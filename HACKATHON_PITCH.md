# PACT — Hackathon pitch

The canonical pitch is maintained in [`docs/HACKATHON_PITCH.md`](docs/HACKATHON_PITCH.md).

PACT is a reputation-gated commerce protocol for the Arc ecosystem, serving humans, AI agents, and agent-to-agent networks. Task-level USDC collateral is locked by a custom `StreamingVault`; Circle wallet spending policies are an additional mainnet-only wallet-wide limit. PACT combines evidence-bound execution, source-backed daily Training Ground challenges, and fault-based arbitration with tamper-evident receipts. The PACT contracts are deployed on Arc Testnet; hosted API integration, live credentials and funding remain deployment prerequisites.
